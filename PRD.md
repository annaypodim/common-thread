# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## COMMON-THREAD: High School Applications Advisor + Essay Reviewer

### Overview
- This product is a web-based tool designed to help students strategically plan and write stronger college and summer program applications.
- The platform provides structured feedback on essays, analyzes extracurricular activities to identify a student’s strongest narrative angle, and organizes college research and application materials in a unified dashboard.
- Unlike traditional AI writing tools, this system does NOT rewrite student essays. Instead, it preserves the student’s voice while offering targeted feedback on structure, storytelling clarity, and narrative strength.
- The goal is to create an application strategy platform that combines the functionality of tools like Notion, Google Docs, and Grammarly into a single workflow optimized for applicants.

### Goals
- Help students identify their strongest application narrative.
- Provide actionable feedback on essays without generating rewritten content.
- Simplify the organization of college research, essay drafts, and application timelines.
- Create a visually intuitive interface for planning applications and writing essays.

### Targeted Users
- High school juniors and seniors applying to college.
- Students applying to summer programs (COSMOS, RSI, precollegiate programs, etc.).

**Future Secondary Users**
- College counselors.
- Teachers mentoring student applications!

### Core Features
#### Application Angle Analyzer
- Allow students to identify the strongest narrative angle for their application.
- **Input:** activities, awards, interests, intended major.
- **Output Example:**
  - **Primary Narrative Theme:** Environmental data + civic impact.
  - **Supporting Evidence:**
    - Air quality research.
    - Climate interview blog.
    - Environmental internships.

#### Voice-Preserving Essay Feedback Tool
- Students paste their essay into an editor and receive structured feedback.
- Tool will not rewrite the essay or give wording recommendations.
- **Feedback categories include:** narrative clarity, reflection depth, structure and flow, word count optimization, strongest sentences, weak/repetitive sections.

#### “Why This College” Research Engine
- Students enter a college name and the system generates relevant research tailored to their interests.
- System identifies: academic programs, professors/labs, clubs and research initiatives.
- **Output Example:**
  - Stanford highlights relevant to you:
    - Atmosphere Energy Program.
    - Sustainable AI Lab.
    - Stanford Data Science Initiative.
  - Possible essay angles:
    - climate modeling.
    - data-driven environmental policy.

#### College Application Dashboard
- A centralized interface for organizing application materials.
- Each college page includes: deadlines, essay prompts, essay drafts, interview preparation notes, research notes, pros and cons.
- Dashboard is inspired by Notion-style organization.

### Product Vision
- The long-term goal is to build a comprehensive operating system for academically rigorous applicants, helping students plan their strategies, write authentic essays, and organize their application processes on a single platform.
